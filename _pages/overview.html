An overview of TransferVox

TransferVox helps price event tickets.

Introduction

In this 2 minute video, hear from our co-founder, Judson Moore, on why we built TransferVox and how it works.

Problem

How do artists determine what they should charge for a live event? Traditional sources of data for determining ticket prices leads to significant revenue leakage.

65%+

Concert tickets unsold

20%

Tickets resold to the secondary market

$15B

Revenue lost to the secondary market

Our Solution

Ask people what they are willing to pay. TransferVox conducts price discovery through a first-price sealed-bid auction. Ticket buyers bid what they are willing to pay and don’t know what others bid. Academic economic research supports that tickets sold at auction:

Revenue roughly doubles

Price discovery substantically improves

Secondary market margins evaporate
